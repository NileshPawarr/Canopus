Welcome to the World Of Canopus Holidays !!

In the year 1987, Mrs. Neeta N Pathak the founder of YASH TOURS AND TRAVELS had the vision to create affordable and luxurious travel packages for all age groups. Her intentions has presented us with the wonderful opportunities to explore new avenues in tourism. She brought together a great team, which worked effortlessly. As pioneers of innovative holiday ideas, international tours and educational tours, hence company has been rebranded as “Canopus Holidays”.

Canopus Holidays a leading education travel organisation under guidance of Mr. Kunal Pathak the President, an educational excursionist having vast on field travel experience within India and across the globe. Being a educational tour operator, we have organised multiple educational tours within international and domestic arena.
